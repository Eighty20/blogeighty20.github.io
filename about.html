Why we started a blog

John Tukey once said "The best thing about being a statistician is that you get to play in everyone's backyard". This is very true within an analytics consultancy, where problems that present themselves sometimes end to soon. What we mean by this statement is, sometimes further exploration into a technique or interesting model is not warranted as the scope of the project does not allow it. This is where a analytics blog comes in. This acts as an incubator for further investigation into techniques which interest our analyst/ data scientists.

Who should read this

Anyone who wants to learn something new, explore techniques or just read about a fun application of a workhorse model. The team consists out of individuals from quantitative fields such as economics, statistics and/or mathematics. This gives a plethora of scope on topics. On here you will find anything from book reviews to useful function which could ease your daily coding life
